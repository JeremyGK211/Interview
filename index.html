Why do you want to be a designer?

I want to be a graphic designer because I want to used my creative skills in my everyday life. I love the idea of working with different kinds of clients and also think that being a graphic designer can provide me with everyday problems and challenges that I will enjoy solving.

What makes you nervous about becoming a designer?

What makes me nervous is that graphic design is a very broad term. There are a lot of different fields and directions of which I can enter, and as of now I am not 100% positive where I will end up in the field.

What excites you about design?

What excited me about design is that it is limitless. It is continuously evolving. I also love that it can communicate in so many different ways.

Why do you want to be a designer?

I want to be a graphic designer because I want to used my creative skills in my everyday life. I love the idea of working with different kinds of clients and also think that being a graphic designer can provide me with everyday problems and challenges that I will enjoy solving.

What are your strengths?

I think my strengths are my analog work. I am a good illustrator.

What are some areas of opportunity to work on?

Some areas that I need to work on are my technical skills in many of the adobe programs. Though I have gotten much better, I still find that there are times when I have a good idea, but simply am limited by my skills in these programs.

What aspects of graphic design do you gravitate towards?

I gravitate towards illustration. I am also finding that I really like information graphics.

What are the roles and responsibilities of a graphic designer in society?

In my opinion a graphic designer has a very large roll in society. Graphic Designers control almost everything we see and therefore can influence the opinion and attitudes of our consumers. In my opinion society as a whole can be shaped and formed through graphic designers.

When/where/why are you at your most creative/productive?

I am at my most creative in the morning, after I have done a workout. I am most productive when I am out of the house and at school or a coffee shop to do work. This is because I have the most energy in the morning after I have worked out. My home for me is my place of rest, and I try not to do any work at home. Instead I get out of the house and work elsewhere.
